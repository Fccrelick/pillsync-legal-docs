How do I set up medication reminders?
Open PillSync, tap the "+" button, enter your medication details, and set your preferred reminder times. The app will send notifications at the scheduled times.
How do I sync my data across devices?
PillSync automatically syncs your medication data across all your Apple devices using iCloud. Make sure you're signed in to the same iCloud account on all devices.
Can I use PillSync without internet?
Yes! PillSync works completely offline. Your medication data is stored locally on your device, and reminders will work without an internet connection.
How do I upgrade to Premium?
Go to Settings > Premium to view subscription options. Premium removes ads and unlocks unlimited medications.
I'm not receiving notifications. What should I do?
Check that notifications are enabled in iOS Settings > Notifications > PillSync. Also ensure that Do Not Disturb is not blocking your medication reminders.
How do I delete my data?
You can delete individual medications by swiping left on them, or delete all data by removing the PillSync app from your device.
