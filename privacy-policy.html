Privacy Policy for PillSync

Last Updated: January 17, 2025

Introduction

PillSync ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application PillSync (the "App").

Important Medical Disclaimer: PillSync is a medication reminder tool and is not intended for medical emergencies. Always consult with healthcare professionals for medical advice and treatment decisions.

Information We Collect

Health & Fitness Data (Linked to Your Identity)

  • Medication Information: Names, dosages, schedules, and notes about your medications
  • Reminder Settings: Times, frequencies, and notification preferences
  • Usage: This health data is stored on your device and linked to your identity through device storage and iCloud sync
  • Not Used for Tracking: Your health data is NEVER used for advertising tracking or shared with third parties

User Content (Linked to Your Identity)

  • Medication Notes: Optional text notes you add to your medications
  • Personal Records: These notes are stored with your medication data and linked to your device identity

Device Identifiers (Used for Advertising & Analytics)

  • Advertising ID: Used by Google AdMob for targeted advertising
  • Device Information: iOS version, app version, device type
  • Purpose: Third-party advertising and app analytics only

Usage Data (Product Interactions)

  • App Interactions: Taps, navigation, feature usage
  • Advertising Data: Ad views, clicks, impressions
  • Purpose: Third-party advertising, analytics, and app functionality improvement

Technical Data

  • Crash Data: Automatic crash logs for bug fixes
  • Performance Data: App launch times, memory usage, performance metrics
  • Purpose: Analytics and app functionality improvement

How We Use Your Information

Core Functionality

  • Medication Reminders: Store and manage your medication schedules
  • Notifications: Send timely reminders for your medications
  • Data Sync: Synchronize your data across your devices via iCloud (optional)

App Improvement

  • Analytics: Understand how users interact with the app
  • Crash Reports: Identify and fix technical issues
  • Feature Development: Improve existing features and develop new ones

Advertising (Free Users Only)

  • Personalized Ads: Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Ad Performance: Track ad effectiveness and user engagement
  • Revenue Generation: Support free app usage through ad revenue

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Primary Storage: All medication data is stored locally on your device
  • Encryption: Data is encrypted using iOS security features
  • Access Control: Only you can access your medication data

Cloud Sync (Optional)

  • iCloud Integration: Securely sync data across your Apple devices
  • Apple's Security: Utilizes Apple's iCloud security infrastructure
  • User Control: You can disable cloud sync at any time

Data Sharing

We DO NOT Share

  • Medication Information: Never shared with third parties
  • Personal Health Data: Kept strictly confidential
  • Individual User Data: No selling or sharing of personal information

We MAY Share

  • Aggregated Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics for app improvement
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or legal process
  • Business Transfers: In case of company sale or merger (with user notification)

Your Rights and Choices

Data Control

  • Access: View all your stored medication data within the app
  • Modification: Edit or delete any medication information
  • Deletion: Delete all data by removing the app

Privacy Settings

  • Notifications: Enable/disable medication reminders
  • Cloud Sync: Enable/disable iCloud synchronization
  • Ad Preferences: Limited control through iOS settings

Premium Features

  • Ad-Free Experience: Remove all advertisements
  • Enhanced Privacy: No advertising tracking for Premium users
  • Advanced Features: Additional privacy-focused features

Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

  • Email: support@pillsync.app
  • Subject: Privacy Policy Inquiry
  • Response Time: Within 48 hours

Note: This privacy policy is designed to be compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and Apple's App Store requirements. By using PillSync, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.
